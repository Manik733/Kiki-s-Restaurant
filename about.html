Our Story

Ravish Singh, our talented founder began his journey in the bustling kitchens of a renowned bakery in Alberta, where he honed his skills, experimenting with flavors and perfecting the delicate art of pastry-making. Fuelled by his ambition to bring something unique to the Vancouver's vibrant food scene, Ravish meticulously curated recipes, blending traditional techniques with contemporary twists. The dream took shape, and Kiki's Bakery was born. In 2012, Ravish opened the first charming storefront in the heart of downtown Vancouver, a cozy space that would soon become a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike. Now Kiki's Bakery has also opened its doors in Calgary and Alberta.
